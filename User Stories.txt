As a customer I want...
...to search events
...to select one or more seats
...to buy one or more tickets
...to receive a confirmation of my order
...to receive a digital ticket
...subscribe to be notified about entertainers

As a booker I want to...
...create events
  ...that recur
  ...with multiple showtimes
  ...with different prices
...create entertainers
  ...that don't already exist and are verified
...create promotions
...view analytics for my events

As an entertainer I want to...
...verify my account
...modify my images and bio
...embed my profile in other websites
...add events from non-managed venues
...view analytics for my events

As a receptionist I want to...
...change tickets and account for difference in ticket price
...purchase tickets on behalf of someone (in-person or phone)
...view inventory for events

As a manager I want to...
...cancel an event and auto-refund with email confirmation all pre-purchased tickets
...change tickets and account for difference in ticket price
...upload menus
...view analytics
...adjust fee distribution
...allocate comps to entertainers, promoters, and customers

As a promoter I want to...
...send bulk emails for show notifications and upcoming events

As an owner I want to...
...verify venue account
...modify venue images/tours/bio
...update venue information
...view analytics for all venue events

As an admin I want to...
...create users and modify roles
